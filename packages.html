About Kabini Packages

Set on a coffee plantation, this serene, posh resort is 7 km from Chikkamagaluru's town centre and 9 km from Chikkamagaluru rail station.
Bright, upscale villas with hardwood floors feature satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, living/dining areas, and tea and coffeemaking facilities, plus gardens with private pools or Jacuzzis. Room service is available.
